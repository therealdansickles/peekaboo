## Teacher Onboarding Email

**Subject Line Options:**
1. Getting started with Peekaboo — your step-by-step guide
2. Welcome to Peekaboo! Here's how to share photos with parents
3. [Teacher Name], you're all set up on Peekaboo

**Recommended Subject:** Getting started with Peekaboo — your step-by-step guide

---

**Email Body:**

Hi [Teacher First Name],

Welcome to Peekaboo! You're now set up to share photos with parents at [School Name]. Here's everything you need to know.

---

**Step 1: Sign in**

Tap the link below to sign in (no password needed):

[Sign In to Peekaboo →]

We'll send you a magic link whenever you need to sign in. Just tap and you're in.

---

**Step 2: Upload photos**

1. Tap the **+** button or "Upload Photos"
2. Select photos from your camera roll
3. Choose which children appear in each photo

That's it! Parents will be notified automatically.

---

**Step 3: Tag children**

When you upload, you'll see a list of children in your classroom. Tap each child who appears in the photo. Parents will only see photos where their child is tagged.

**Tip:** You can tag multiple children in the same photo. Each family will see it in their timeline.

---

**A few helpful things:**

• **Photos are private.** Each parent only sees photos of their own child. You control who sees what.

• **We remove location data.** Every photo is automatically stripped of GPS and other metadata. Parents can't see where photos were taken.

• **Upload from any device.** Use your phone, tablet, or computer — whatever's easiest.

• **Parents are already invited.** [School Name] has added all your families. They'll get notified when you share.

---

**Quick tips for great photos:**

1. Get down to their level — photos at eye height feel warmer
2. Capture moments, not poses — candid beats staged
3. Include hands-on activities — parents love seeing their child engaged
4. Share throughout the day — don't wait until pickup time

---

**Need help?**

Reply to this email or visit peekaboo.app/help. We're here for you.

Happy sharing,

The Peekaboo Team

## Email Design Notes

### Brand Guidelines

- **Primary color:** Violet (#7C3AED)
- **Tone:** Warm, trustworthy, simple
- **Reading level:** Aim for 6th-grade readability
- **Length:** Keep emails scannable — use bullet points and short paragraphs

### Visual Elements

- Logo at top (Peekaboo wordmark)
- Single-column layout, mobile-optimized
- Buttons in violet (#7C3AED) with white text
- Ample white space between sections
- Footer with unsubscribe link and mailing address (required by CAN-SPAM)

### Personalization Variables

| Variable | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| `[Parent First Name]` | Parent's first name |
| `[Child Name]` | Child's first name |
| `[School Name]` | Preschool/daycare name |
| `[Teacher First Name]` | Teacher's first name |

### Technical Notes

- Use plain text version as fallback
- Test rendering on Gmail, Apple Mail, Outlook
- Magic link should have 24-hour expiration
- Track open rates and click rates for optimization

## Implementation Notes

### Recommended Email Platforms

| Platform | Pricing | Best For |
|----------|---------|----------|
| **Loops** | Free up to 1K contacts | Modern, developer-friendly |
| **Resend** | Free up to 3K emails/mo | Transactional + marketing |
| **Postmark** | $15/mo for 10K emails | Deliverability-focused |
| **SendGrid** | Free up to 100/day | High volume |

### Automation Triggers

1. **Signup events** → Welcome emails
2. **Time-based** → Nurture sequence
3. **Behavior-based** → Re-engagement (no login/no upload)
4. **Milestone-based** → Celebration emails
5. **Transactional** → Photo notifications, magic links

### Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Open rate | 40%+ | Higher for transactional |
| Click rate | 15%+ | For emails with CTAs |
| Unsubscribe rate | <0.5% | Monitor for list health |
| Reply rate | 2%+ | Indicates engagement |
