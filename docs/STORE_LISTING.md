### Subtitle (30 characters max)

### Short Description (80 characters)

### Long Description (4000 characters max)

**See your child's day. Know their photos are safe.**

Peekaboo is the secure photo-sharing app built for preschools that care about privacy. Teachers snap photos. Parents see them instantly. Every image is protected with bank-level security.

**Why parents love Peekaboo:**

• **Daily photo updates** — See what your child did today, not next week
• **Privacy you can trust** — We strip location data from every photo automatically
• **No passwords to remember** — Sign in with a simple magic link sent to your email
• **One place for everything** — All your child's photos in one beautiful timeline
• **Free forever for families** — Parents never pay a cent

**Why teachers love Peekaboo:**

• **Upload in seconds** — Snap, select, share. No complicated steps
• **Tag children instantly** — Parents only see photos of their own kids
• **No per-child fees** — Flat pricing that doesn't punish growing classrooms
• **Works on any device** — iPhone, iPad, Android, or web browser
• **Focus on what matters** — Photo sharing done right, nothing more

**Security parents deserve:**

Your child's photos are precious. We treat them that way.

• All photos are encrypted in transit and at rest
• Location metadata (EXIF data) is automatically removed
• Each photo link expires and cannot be shared
• Parents can only see their own children
• We never sell your data. Ever.

**Simple for everyone:**

1. Teacher uploads photos and tags children
2. Parents receive a notification
3. Parents view and download photos securely
4. That's it. No training required.

**What makes Peekaboo different:**

Unlike complicated all-in-one daycare apps, Peekaboo does one thing perfectly: secure photo sharing. No billing features you don't need. No attendance tracking you'll never use. Just a beautiful, simple way to share the moments that matter.

**Free for parents. Affordable for schools.**

Parents always use Peekaboo for free. Schools can start with our free tier (up to 25 children) and upgrade as they grow. No surprises. No per-child fees.

**Your preschool's photos, protected.**

Download Peekaboo today and give parents peace of mind.
