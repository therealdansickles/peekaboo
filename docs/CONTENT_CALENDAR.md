# Peekaboo 90-Day Content Calendar

**Last Updated**: March 14, 2026
**Prepared by**: Growth Agent (Paperclip)

---

## Overview

This document provides a structured 90-day content plan for Peekaboo's launch and growth phase. It builds on the first-week calendar in SOCIAL_MEDIA_PROFILES.md and extends it through the critical first three months.

**Goals**:
- Month 1: Build awareness, drive initial downloads (Target: 200 downloads)
- Month 2: Generate reviews, establish credibility (Target: 25 reviews)
- Month 3: Scale referrals, convert free to paid (Target: 5 paying schools)

---

All content should align with one of these four pillars:

| **Parent Connection** | Seeing your child's day, emotional moments | 30% |

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

- **Frequency**: 2-3x/week

## Content Bank (Pre-Written Posts)

### Privacy Posts

**Post 1: EXIF Data**
```
When you take a photo with your phone, it records:
- Your exact GPS location
- Date and time
- Device information
- Sometimes your altitude

All of this goes with every photo you text or email.

Peekaboo strips it automatically. Every photo. Every time.
```

**Post 2: Link Expiration**
```
That photo you emailed in 2019?

The link still works.
Anyone with it can view it.
Forever.

Peekaboo photo links expire in 15 minutes. If someone forwards a link, it won't work.

Small detail. Big protection.
```

**Post 3: Row-Level Security**
```
"Row-level security" sounds technical.

Here's what it means in plain English:

The database itself enforces who can see what. It's not just app logic—it's how the data is stored.

Even if someone hacked our app, they couldn't see photos they shouldn't see.

That's real security.
```

### Efficiency Posts

**Post 1: Time Comparison**
```
Before Peekaboo:
- Take photos
- Open email
- Attach photos
- Remember who should get what
- Hope you didn't include the wrong kid
- Send 5 separate emails
- Hope they don't forward it

After Peekaboo:
- Take photos
- Tag kids
- Done

30 seconds vs 20 minutes.
```

**Post 2: No Passwords**
```
Teachers don't need another password.
Parents don't need another app login.

Peekaboo uses magic links.

Enter email → Get link → Tap link → You're in.

No passwords to forget.
No resets to manage.
Just photos.
```

### Connection Posts

**Post 1: How Was Your Day**
```
"How was your day?"
"Fine."
"What did you do?"
"I don't know."

Every parent knows this conversation.

Peekaboo changes it.

"I see you painted a butterfly today!"
"YEAH! And then we..."

Photos unlock conversations. That's why this matters.
```

**Post 2: Working Parent Guilt**
```
8 hours apart from your kid.

You can't be at the art project.
You can't be at snack time.
You can't see their face when they master something new.

But you can see the photo—seconds after it happens.

Peekaboo doesn't replace being there. But it makes the distance smaller.
```
