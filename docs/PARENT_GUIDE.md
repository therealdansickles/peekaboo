# Peekaboo Parent Guide

Welcome to Peekaboo! See what your child is up to at school, securely.

## Getting Started

### Signing In

1. Open Peekaboo on your phone or go to https://peekaboo.photos
2. Tap **"I'm a Parent"**
3. Enter the email address your school has on file
4. Check your email for a magic link
5. Tap the link to sign in (no password needed!)

**Tip**: Use the same email your school has in their records. If you're not sure, ask your child's teacher.

### First Time Setup

When you first sign in:
- Your account is automatically linked to your child
- This linking is done by your school's administrator
- You'll only see photos where your child is tagged

## Your Dashboard

After signing in, you'll see your **Parent View**:

- **Child banner**: Shows your child's name and classroom
- **Photo count**: Number of photos available today
- **Security banner**: Confirms your connection is encrypted

### If You Have Multiple Children

If you have more than one child enrolled:
1. Look for the child selector at the top
2. Tap a child's name to switch views
3. Each child's photos are shown separately

## Viewing Photos

### Photo Feed

Your photo feed shows all photos where your child appears:
- Most recent photos at the top
- Photos include captions from teachers (when provided)
- Tap a photo to view it larger

### Photo Security

For your child's safety:
- **Photo URLs expire in 15 minutes** - This prevents sharing links
- **Location data is removed** - No GPS info in photos
- **Encrypted connection** - Secure from your device to our servers

### Refreshing Photos

New photos appear automatically, but you can also:
1. Tap the refresh icon (circular arrows)
2. Or pull down on the photo list to refresh

## Favorite Photos

### Marking Favorites

To save a favorite photo:
1. Tap the heart icon on any photo
2. The heart fills in to show it's favorited
3. Tap again to unfavorite

### Why Favorite?

- Mark your favorite moments to find later
- Favorites are saved to your account
- Only you can see your favorites

**Note**: Due to security, you cannot download photos directly. This protects all children's privacy.

## Privacy & Security

### What You Can See

- **Only your child's photos**: You see photos where your child is tagged
- **No other children**: Even in group photos, you only see ones with your child
- **Real-time updates**: New photos appear as teachers share them

### What Keeps Photos Safe

| Protection | What It Does |
|------------|--------------|
| Row Level Security | Ensures you only see your child |
| Expiring URLs | Photo links stop working after 15 minutes |
| EXIF Stripping | Location and camera data removed |
| Magic Link Auth | No password to guess or steal |

### Your Data

- **Email**: Used only for sign-in
- **Favorites**: Stored securely, only you can see them
- **Photos**: You can view but not download (protects all children)

## Troubleshooting

### "No Children Linked"

This means your school hasn't linked your account yet:
1. Contact your child's teacher or school office
2. Confirm they have your correct email address
3. Ask them to link your account to your child

### No Photos Showing

- Your child may not have been in today's photos yet
- Pull down to refresh
- Check if you're viewing the correct child (if multiple)

### Magic Link Not Working

- Links expire after 1 hour
- Make sure you're clicking the most recent link
- Request a new link by signing in again
- Check that you're opening the link on the same device

### "Sign in failed"

- Make sure you're using the email your school has on file
- Check for typos in your email address
- Contact your school if problems persist

## Multiple Devices

You can sign in on multiple devices:
- Phone, tablet, and computer all work
- Each device needs to sign in separately
- Magic links work on any device

## Getting Help

### In-App Help

Tap the **?** icon in the top right corner for help while using the app.

### Contact Your School

For issues with:
- Linking your account
- Missing photos
- Account access

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I download photos?**
A: Not directly, to protect all children's privacy. You can take a screenshot if needed.

**Q: Why do I only see some group photos?**
A: Teachers tag specific children in each photo. You only see photos where your child is tagged.

**Q: Can grandparents get access?**
A: Ask your school! They can link additional family members to your child.

**Q: Is this app free?**
A: Yes! Peekaboo is free for parents. Schools pay for the service.
