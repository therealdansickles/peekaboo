# Peekaboo Teacher Guide

Welcome to Peekaboo! This guide will help you share photos with families securely.

## Getting Started

### Signing In

1. Open Peekaboo on your phone or go to https://peekaboo.photos
2. Tap **"I'm a Teacher"**
3. Enter your school email address
4. Check your email for a magic link
5. Tap the link to sign in (no password needed!)

**Tip**: Check your spam folder if you don't see the email within a minute.

### Your Dashboard

After signing in, you'll see your **Teacher Dashboard**:

- **Classroom banner**: Shows your current classroom name and school
- **Photos tab**: View and share photos
- **Messages tab**: Coming soon!
- **Your Class section**: Shows all children in your classroom

## Sharing Photos

### Taking or Selecting Photos

1. Tap the **"Share Photos"** button (camera icon)
2. Choose an option:
   - **Take Photo**: Use your phone's camera
   - **Choose from Library**: Select existing photos

### Tagging Children

After selecting a photo:

1. You'll see a list of children in your classroom
2. Tap each child who appears in the photo
3. Selected children show a checkmark
4. **Important**: Only tag children who are actually in the photo!

### Adding a Caption (Optional)

1. Tap the caption field
2. Write a short description (e.g., "Art project time!")
3. Captions help parents understand the context

### Uploading

1. Review your photo and tags
2. Tap **"Upload"**
3. Wait for the upload to complete
4. The photo will appear in your Recent Photos

## Privacy & Security

### What Happens When You Upload

1. **EXIF data is removed**: Location, camera info, and other metadata are stripped
2. **Photos are compressed**: Large images are optimized for mobile viewing
3. **Secure storage**: Photos are stored in encrypted cloud storage
4. **Signed URLs**: Photo links expire after 15 minutes

### Who Can See Photos

- **Only tagged children's parents** can see the photo
- If you tag Emma, only Emma's parents see it
- If you tag Emma and Liam, both families see it
- Other parents cannot see photos of children they're not linked to

### Best Practices

- **Always verify tags**: Double-check which children are in the photo
- **Avoid sensitive content**: No photos during bathroom/changing times
- **Get consent**: Follow your school's photo consent policies
- **Group shots**: Only tag children whose parents have given consent

## Managing Photos

### Viewing Recent Photos

Your dashboard shows recently uploaded photos with:
- Thumbnail preview
- Caption (if added)
- Which children are tagged
- Upload date/time

### Deleting Photos

If you need to remove a photo:
1. Find the photo in Recent Photos
2. Tap the delete icon (trash can)
3. Confirm deletion
4. The photo is permanently removed

**Note**: Deleted photos cannot be recovered.

## Multiple Classrooms

If you teach multiple classrooms:

1. Look for the classroom dropdown at the top
2. Tap to switch between classrooms
3. Photos are always uploaded to the currently selected classroom

## Troubleshooting

### "No children in classroom"

- Contact your school admin to add children to your classroom
- Make sure you're signed in with the correct email

### Photos not uploading

- Check your internet connection
- Make sure the photo isn't too large (try a different photo)
- Try closing and reopening the app

### Magic link not working

- Links expire after 1 hour
- Request a new link by signing in again
- Make sure you're clicking the link on the same device

### Can't access camera

- Go to your phone's Settings
- Find Peekaboo in the app list
- Enable Camera permission

## Getting Help

Tap the **?** icon in the top right corner to access in-app help.

For additional support, contact your school administrator.
