# Peekaboo Referral Program

**Last Updated**: March 14, 2026
**Prepared by**: Growth Agent (Paperclip)

---

## Overview

School-to-school referrals are Peekaboo's most powerful growth lever. Teachers move between schools. Directors network at conferences. Parents have friends with kids at other schools. This document outlines a simple, trackable referral program to capture this organic word-of-mouth.

---

## Program Structure

### Simple Referral Mechanic

**Referrer**: Current Peekaboo school (teacher, admin, or director)
**Referee**: New school that signs up via referral

**Reward Structure**:

| Action | Referrer Gets | Referee Gets |
|--------|---------------|--------------|
| New school signs up (free tier) | Thank you email | Welcome to Peekaboo |
| New school uploads 25+ photos | 1 month free upgrade | 1 month free upgrade |
| New school upgrades to paid | 1 month free on current plan | First month free |

### Why This Works

1. **Low friction**: No complex point systems
2. **Value alignment**: Rewards happen after real usage, not just signups
3. **Mutual benefit**: Both schools get something
4. **Delayed gratification**: Encourages referrer to help onboard referee

## Referral Program FAQ

### For Schools

**Q: How do I find my referral code?**
A: Your code is [SCHOOL-NAME]-REF. It's also in your account settings and in our emails to you.

**Q: What do I get when someone uses my code?**
A: When a new school signs up and starts using Peekaboo (25+ photos), you both get 1 free month on your next tier. If they upgrade to paid, you get another free month.

**Q: Is there a limit to how many schools I can refer?**
A: No limit! Refer as many schools as you'd like.

**Q: How long do I have to use my reward?**
A: Rewards don't expire. They're applied to your next billing cycle automatically.

**Q: Can I refer a school I used to work at?**
A: Absolutely! Former colleagues are often the best referrals.

### For Referred Schools

**Q: How do I use a referral code?**
A: Enter it when you create your school account, or email us at hello@peekaboo.photos with the code within 7 days of signup.

**Q: What do I get?**
A: 1 month free on your first upgrade (from Free to Starter, or Starter to Growth).

**Q: The code didn't work—what now?**
A: Email hello@peekaboo.photos with the code and the school that referred you. We'll apply it manually.

## Referral Program Metrics

### Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|--------|------------|--------|
| Referral codes distributed | Schools who have a code | 100% of schools |
| Referral code shares | Schools who shared their code (via email open + click) | 30% of schools |
| Referrals submitted | New schools who entered a code | 10% share rate |
| Referrals activated | Referred schools who uploaded 25+ photos | 70% conversion |
| Referrals upgraded | Referred schools who upgraded to paid | 15% conversion |

### Reporting Cadence

**Weekly**:
- New referrals this week
- Rewards issued
- Top referrers

**Monthly**:
- Referral program ROI
- Referral vs. organic signup comparison
- Top 10 referrers (consider recognizing them)

## Legal Considerations

### Terms to Include

1. Referral rewards are non-transferable and have no cash value
2. Peekaboo reserves the right to modify or terminate the program
3. Fraudulent referrals (self-referrals, fake schools) will be disqualified
4. Rewards apply to future billing; no refunds on past charges
5. Limit of 12 free months per school per year

### Privacy

- Referrer only sees that their code was used, not referee's detailed activity
- Referee sees who referred them (school name only)
- No personal data shared between schools
