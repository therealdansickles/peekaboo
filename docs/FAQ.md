# Peekaboo FAQ

**Last Updated**: March 14, 2026

Frequently asked questions about Peekaboo, organized by audience.

---

## For Parents

### Getting Started

**How do I sign up?**

You don't need to sign up—your school invites you! When your child's school starts using Peekaboo, they'll send you an email invitation. Just tap the link to get started.

**How do I sign in?**

1. Go to peekaboo.photos or open the Peekaboo app
2. Tap "I'm a Parent"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for a magic link
5. Tap the link and you're in!

No password needed—ever.

**What if I don't get the magic link email?**

- Check your spam or junk folder
- Make sure you're using the email address your school has on file
- Try requesting another link (they expire after 1 hour)
- Contact your school to confirm your email is correct in their system

**Can I use Peekaboo on multiple devices?**

Yes! Sign in on your phone, tablet, and computer. Each device needs to sign in separately using a magic link.

---

### Viewing Photos

**Why can I only see some photos?**

You only see photos where your child is specifically tagged by the teacher. This is how we protect every child's privacy—each family only sees their own children.

**How often are new photos shared?**

That's up to your child's teacher! Some share daily, some weekly. When new photos are available, they'll appear automatically in your feed.

**Can I download photos?**

To protect all children's privacy, direct downloads are not available. You can take a screenshot if you'd like to keep a photo.

**Why do photo links expire?**

Photo links expire after 15 minutes as a security measure. This means if a link is shared or intercepted, it quickly becomes useless. You can always view photos again by signing in.

---

### Privacy & Security

**What personal information does Peekaboo collect?**

We collect only what's necessary:
- Your email address (for sign-in)
- Your favorites (which photos you've hearted)
- Basic usage data (to improve the app)

We never sell your data.

**How do you protect my child's photos?**

Multiple layers of protection:
1. **Row-Level Security**: You can only access photos of your own child—enforced at the database level
2. **EXIF Stripping**: Location, camera, and timestamp data removed before storage
3. **Signed URLs**: Photo links expire after 15 minutes
4. **Encrypted Storage**: Photos encrypted at rest and in transit
5. **No Passwords**: Magic links eliminate password theft risks

**Can other parents see my child?**

Only if your child is tagged in a photo AND the other parent's child is also tagged in that same photo. In that case, both families would see the group photo. Teachers are trained to only tag children whose parents have given consent.

**Who owns the photos?**

Your school owns the photos. Peekaboo provides the secure storage and viewing platform. We don't claim any rights to your family's images.

**How do I change my email address?**

**Can grandparents or other family members have access?**

Yes! Ask your school to add additional family members. Each person gets their own account linked to your child.

**How do I delete my account?**

Contact your school administrator to be removed from Peekaboo. Alternatively, email support@peekaboo.app with your request.

## For Teachers

### Getting Started

**How do I get access to Peekaboo?**

Your school administrator adds you to the system. You'll receive a welcome email with instructions to sign in.

**What devices can I use?**

Any device with a web browser or the Peekaboo app:
- iPhone or iPad
- Android phone or tablet
- Computer (any browser)

**Can I use my personal phone?**

Yes! Peekaboo works on any device. Photos you upload are stored securely in the cloud, not on your device.

---

### Uploading Photos

**How do I upload photos?**

1. Tap "Share Photos" or the + button
2. Take a new photo or select from your camera roll
3. Tap each child who appears in the photo
4. Add an optional caption
5. Tap Upload

**Can I upload multiple photos at once?**

Yes! Select multiple photos from your camera roll. You'll tag children for each photo before uploading.

**What happens to location data in my photos?**

We automatically remove all EXIF data (location, camera info, timestamps) before storing photos. This happens instantly and automatically—you don't need to do anything.

**How long does upload take?**

Usually a few seconds per photo. Large photos are automatically compressed for faster upload and viewing.

---

### Tagging Children

**Why do I need to tag children?**

Tagging determines which families see each photo. If you tag Emma, only Emma's family sees the photo. This is how we protect every child's privacy.

**What if a child isn't in my list?**

Contact your school administrator. They need to add the child to your classroom in Peekaboo.

**Can I tag the same child in multiple photos?**

Absolutely! Tag each child in every photo they appear in. Families love seeing all their child's moments.

**What if I make a tagging mistake?**

You can delete the photo and re-upload it with correct tags. Contact your administrator if you need help.

---

### Managing Photos

**Can I delete a photo after uploading?**

Yes. Find the photo in your recent uploads and tap the delete icon. Deleted photos are permanently removed.

**Can parents see old photos?**

Yes, all photos remain viewable unless deleted. Families can scroll back through their child's entire photo history.

**Is there a storage limit?**

Your school's plan determines storage limits. Most schools have plenty of room. Contact your administrator if you're concerned about limits.

---

### Multiple Classrooms

**Can I teach multiple classrooms?**

Yes! If you're assigned to multiple classrooms, you'll see a dropdown to switch between them. Make sure you've selected the correct classroom before uploading.

**Do I need separate accounts?**

No. One account can be linked to multiple classrooms.

## For School Administrators

### Setup & Onboarding

**How do I get started with Peekaboo?**

1. Sign up at peekaboo.photos
2. Create your school profile
3. Add classrooms
4. Add children to each classroom
5. Invite teachers (they receive email invitations)
6. Link parents to their children

**How long does setup take?**

Most schools are fully set up in 30-60 minutes. Larger schools with many classrooms may take a bit longer.

**Can I import our roster?**

Currently, children and parents are added manually. CSV import is planned for a future update.

### Managing Users

**How do I add a teacher?**

In your admin dashboard, go to Teachers → Add Teacher. Enter their email address and assign them to one or more classrooms.

**How do I add a parent?**

In your admin dashboard, go to Parents → Add Parent. Enter their email and link them to their child(ren).

**How do I remove someone?**

Teachers and parents can be removed from the admin dashboard. Removed users immediately lose access.

**What if a teacher leaves mid-year?**

Remove them from the system. Their previously uploaded photos remain—only their access is revoked.

## General Questions

**What makes Peekaboo different from other apps?**

We focus exclusively on secure photo sharing. We're not an all-in-one platform with billing, attendance, and messaging bolted on. We do one thing well: help parents see their child's day safely.

**Why "Peekaboo"?**

Like the game you play with babies—revealing something wonderful and watching their face light up. That's what we're building: a way for parents to peek into their child's day and feel connected.

**Is Peekaboo available outside the US?**

Currently, we're focused on US schools. International availability is planned for future expansion.

**How do I give feedback or request features?**

Email us at feedback@peekaboo.app! We read every message and use your input to shape what we build next.

## Troubleshooting

**The app shows a blank screen.**

- Try closing and reopening the app
- Check your internet connection
- Update to the latest version of the app

**Photos won't load.**

- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Photo links expire after 15 minutes—sign in again if needed

**I can't upload photos.**

- Check your internet connection
- Make sure the app has camera/photo library permissions
- Try a different photo (very large images may fail)
- Close and reopen the app

**Magic link says "expired" or "invalid."**

- Links expire after 1 hour
- Request a new link by signing in again
- Make sure you're clicking the most recent link (check for multiple emails)

**I'm not seeing my child's photos.**

- Confirm you're signed in with the email your school has on file
- Your child may not have been tagged in recent photos yet
- Contact your school to verify your account is linked correctly
