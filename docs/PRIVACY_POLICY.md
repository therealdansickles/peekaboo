# Peekaboo Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: March 14, 2026

---

## Overview

Peekaboo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of children, parents, and teachers who use our preschool photo-sharing service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Peekaboo (the "Service").

**Our Core Privacy Principles:**
- We collect only what's necessary to provide the Service
- We never sell your data or your children's photos
- We automatically remove location data from all photos
- Parents only see photos of their own children
- We use encryption to protect all data

---

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign up for Peekaboo, we collect:
- **Email address** — Used for magic link authentication and notifications
- **Role** (teacher, parent, or administrator) — To provide the appropriate app experience
- **School affiliation** — To connect you with your school's photos

### Photos and Content

When teachers upload photos:
- **Photo images** — Stored securely and encrypted
- **Child tags** — To ensure parents see photos of their own children
- **Upload timestamp** — To organize photos chronologically

**Important**: We automatically strip EXIF metadata (including GPS location, device information, and camera settings) from every photo before storage. This protects children's location privacy.

### Usage Information

We collect limited usage data to improve the Service:
- **App interactions** — Which features you use (e.g., photo views, uploads)
- **Error reports** — To fix bugs and improve reliability
- **Device type** — To optimize the app for your device

We do NOT track:
- Detailed browsing history
- Location data
- Contacts or address books
- Any data outside the Peekaboo app

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

1. **Provide the Service** — Authenticate users, store photos, and deliver them to authorized parents
2. **Send notifications** — Alert parents when new photos are available
3. **Improve the app** — Fix bugs and add features based on usage patterns
4. **Provide support** — Respond to your questions and issues

We do NOT use your information to:
- Target advertising
- Sell to third parties
- Train AI models (beyond core product functionality)
- Create marketing profiles

---

## How We Protect Your Information

### Technical Safeguards

- **Encryption in transit** — All data transmitted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
- **Encryption at rest** — Photos and data encrypted in our database
- **Signed URLs** — Photo links expire after 15 minutes and cannot be shared
- **Row-Level Security** — Database enforces that parents only access their children's photos
- **EXIF stripping** — Location and device metadata removed from every photo

### Access Controls

- Magic link authentication (no passwords that can be stolen)
- Role-based access (teachers, parents, administrators see different data)
- Parents can only view photos of children linked to their account

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Peekaboo is designed for use in preschool and childcare settings. We take children's privacy seriously:

### What We Collect About Children

- **First name and classroom** — Used by teachers to tag photos
- **Photos containing the child** — Shared only with authorized parents

### What We Do NOT Collect About Children

- Last names (not required)
- Dates of birth
- Home addresses
- Any direct contact information
- Location data

### Parental Control

- Parents must be invited by a school administrator
- Parents only see photos of their own child(ren)
- Parents can request deletion of their child's photos

### School Responsibility

Schools using Peekaboo are responsible for:
- Obtaining appropriate consent from parents for photo sharing
- Managing which teachers and parents have access
- Complying with their local regulations regarding children's data

---

## Data Retention and Deletion

### How Long We Keep Data

- **Photos** — Retained as long as your school uses Peekaboo, unless deleted by teachers
- **Account information** — Retained while you have an active account
- **Usage analytics** — Retained for 12 months, then anonymized

### Your Deletion Rights

You can request data deletion at any time:
- **Parents**: Contact your school administrator to remove your child's photos
- **Teachers/Administrators**: Delete photos and data through the app
- **Account deletion**: Email hello@peekaboo.app to delete your account

Upon account deletion, we will:
- Remove your profile information within 30 days
- Retain photos only as long as needed for other authorized users
- Anonymize any aggregated analytics data
